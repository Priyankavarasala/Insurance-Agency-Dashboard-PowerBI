# 📊 Insurance Agency Performance Dashboard | Power BI

An end-to-end Business Intelligence dashboard developed in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze insurance agency performance, product trends, retention metrics, growth patterns, and quote system efficiency using interactive visualizations and DAX measures.

---

# 📌 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to transform raw insurance business data into meaningful insights that help management:

- Monitor agency performance
- Track premium growth
- Analyze product line contribution
- Measure customer retention
- Evaluate quote system performance
- Support data-driven business decisions

---

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query

- Microsoft Excel
- Data Modeling

# 📂 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides an overall business snapshot with interactive filters.

### KPIs
- Total Written Premium
- Total New Business Premium
- Total Agencies
- Total States
- Average Loss Ratio
- Average Retention Ratio

### Visuals
- Premium by State (Map)
- Premium Trend (Year-wise)
- Top Performing Agencies
- Interactive Slicers

---

## 2️⃣ Product Line Analysis

Analyzes insurance product performance across different years.

### Insights
- Product Line Distribution
- Top Products by Written Premium
- Written Premium Trend
- Product Contribution Analysis

---

## 3️⃣ Retention Ratio Analysis

Evaluates customer retention performance across agencies and states.

### Insights
- Retention Ratio by State
- Top 10 Agencies by Retention Ratio
- State-wise Retention Comparison
- Agency Performance

---

## 4️⃣ Growth Analysis & Quote System Performance

Tracks business growth and quote conversion metrics.

### Insights
- Growth Trend Analysis
- Hit Ratio by Product Line
- Quote System Performance
- Quote Application Analysis
